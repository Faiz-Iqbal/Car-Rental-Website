Step by Step Process to Rent a Car:

STEP 1: CHOOSE YOUR CAR:
We recommend that you choose from the website's list of cheapest deals, as this contains our best value, most readily available models. Alternatively, we can find you a vehicle if you contact any of our agents.
STEP 2: FILL IN AN ONLINE PROPOSAL:
You can fill an online proposal form here on the website. Because the proposal form contains so much information which you may have to look up, it is fully protected on a secure server and security certificate.
STEP 3: SEND THE PROPOSAL FORM TO ME:
When you have made sure you have completed all the information on the proposal form, press the submit button on the online form, or scan and email it. We may require from business customers a copy of your latest accounts or P&L and balance sheet for the benefit of the finance company (it depends on several factors such as credit scoring, number of years trading, etc). From private customers, maybe we need a recent gas/water/electric bill, or bank statement to prove address, to prevent identity fraud. You will always need a copy of your full UK Driving Licence, or passport. We also use several other security measures. We have never had a case of data theft.
STEP 4: WAIT FOR AN ORDER FORM, THEN SIGN IT:
When finance approval has been completed, we will email you an order form for your car. A signed order form commits you to ordering the car. Check it, print it, sign it, indicate your colour choice, then scan/email it back to us. We will then confirm and order the car and confirm the lead time.
STEP 5: SIGN FINANCE DOCUMENTS AND WAIT FOR THE DELIVER OF YOUR CAR:
Usually, we will post (for security reasons) documents to be signed before delivery of your car. These need to be completed and returned, swiftly, usually together with the initial payment. Occasionally, we will arrange delivery of your car with finance documents to be signed at the same time, but this is not typical. More proofs may be required. All this is to protect your identity. Delivery is free, usually to your home or work address, whichever is most convenient.
STEP 6: ENJOY YOUR CAR:
You need to insure your car, but anyone you choose can drive it. Treat it as you would treat your own personal car. You have full UK manufacturer's warranty and whatever breakdown cover the manufacturer applies. Usually, servicing is your own responsibility. With all the money you have saved, book yourself a nice expensive holiday, or buy a diamond ring!
STEP 7:PREPARE FOR, THEN RETURN YOUR CAR AT THE END OF CONTRACT:
Simply, your car needs to be in a condition commensurate with its age and mileage. This is common sense. A useful guide is available in PDF format to read/print here. You just hand the car back. It is collected directly from you at your address.
